# Portfolio / Malt Positioning

## Recommended Title

AI Data Investigator - agent IA d'analyse, prediction et simulation metier

## Short Pitch

Development of an AI analytics agent able to explore a dataset, detect anomalies, generate business insights, train a predictive model, and simulate decision scenarios such as price or marketing budget changes.

## What It Demonstrates

- product thinking, not only model building
- data profiling and statistical reasoning
- machine learning baseline design with explainability
- business storytelling from technical signals
- API and UI separation for future SaaS evolution

## Suggested Demo Narrative

1. Upload the demo dataset.
2. Show the automatic diagnostic and target suggestions.
3. Walk through the strongest insights and anomalies.
4. Train a baseline model on revenue or churn risk.
5. Simulate a scenario such as a price increase or budget uplift.
6. Close with the executive summary and limitations.

## Client-Friendly Framing

This tool helps answer questions like:

- What changed in my data?
- Which variables seem to matter most?
- Can I predict a business outcome from the current data?
- What might happen if I change a key lever?

## Explicit Limits

- no causal claims
- no live SQL connectors in the MVP
- no heavy MLOps layer yet
- no authentication or multi-user workflows yet
