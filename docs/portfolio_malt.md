# Portfolio / Malt Positioning

## Recommended Title

AI Decision Copilot - agent IA d'investigation, prediction, simulation et recommandations business

## Short Pitch

Development of an AI decision copilot able to analyze datasets, detect ranked insights, explain root causes, train predictive models, compare business scenarios, recommend actions, and export a consulting-style report.

## What It Demonstrates

- product thinking with visible business value
- data profiling and investigation automation
- explainable machine learning for decision support
- scenario simulation with guardrails
- AI orchestration through a session-aware copilot
- clean API / UI separation for future SaaS evolution

## Suggested Demo Narrative

1. Open the landing page and sample demo path.
2. Show investigation suggestions and ranked insights.
3. Train the prediction engine on revenue.
4. Compare scenario A vs B.
5. Ask a business question in the Decision Copilot.
6. Export the HTML executive report.

## Client-Friendly Framing

This product helps answer questions like:

- What changed in my data?
- Why did a KPI move?
- Which drivers matter most?
- What may happen if I change a business lever?
- What should I do next?
